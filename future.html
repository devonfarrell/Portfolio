The Future Women in Marvel

See the Women that will be return and entering the Marvel Univesre this Year!

Yelena

She will be taking over the baton from Black Widow and becoming a warrior queen. She is depicted as a spy and the second modern era character to use the Black Widow name and start a new journey.

Sersi

Sersi will star in the Etnerals, she has the ability to manipulate every matter. She is often a socializer that gathers together the Eternals. She has been known to scarfice her self for the common good at all times.

Thena

Thena will star in the Etnerals and has the ability to be a very strong warrior. Thena has held the highest title of Prime Eternal and also a known elaborate socializer.

Shuri

Shuri will star in the new Blank Panther movie leading Wakanda to glory again. Shuri is the princess of Wakanda and the leader of the Wakadan Design Group.

Ajak

Ajak will star in the Enterals and is the spirtual and knowledgeable leader. Ajak purpose in life is to pave the way for Earth's Emergence.

Jane

Jane Foster will star in the upcoming Thor movie as the powerful Mighty Thor. Dr. Foster is one of the world's leading atrophysicists.

Kate Bishop

Kate Bishop will star in Hawkeye and is an archer and martial artist. Kate has leaned on Hawkeye for guideance and support in becoming an elite and important Avenger.

Miss America

The young teenage will become a strong force in Doctor Strange. As a lesbian, she is Marvel's first LGBTQ character to star in an ongoing series as a powerful young teenager.
